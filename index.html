ABOUT US

Shaping Future Leaders Since 1995

Welcome to Bright Horizon Public School

Bright Horizon Public School, established in 1995, is a premier CBSE-affiliated institution located in the heart of Delhi. Our school is committed to providing quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, creativity, and character development.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a holistic approach to education, we prepare students not just for examinations, but for life itself. Our curriculum balances academic rigor with co-curricular activities to ensure all-round development.
